Features

Employee Evaluations Like Never Before

We transformed the way managers quantify and visualize their team's performance, and made it easier than ever.

Getting Started Right Away

Onboarding is a piece of cake with ECF. Just choose your evaluation criteria, or select one of our comprehensive templates, and invite your team members! Boom! Each member can fill out his or her evaluations, complete with rankings and comments, directly on our website. That's it, no downloads or software installations. Easier than ever before.

Invite Your Team Members

Empower your team members to take accountability for their evaluations. ECF allows your to make assessments available to each employee every month or quarter, along with any comments from management. This allows your employees to which areas they are excelling in and where they have room for improvement. Further, your team members become aware of and can inquire about any particular marks or comments.

View and Make Evaluations Available Over Time

Be in the know about how each member of your team is doing. Analyze how each person performs compared to the team average in each area, from performance, to conduct, to spirit. Identify who your strongest performing teammates are, and which are in need of additional training or guidance. ECF puts and interactive and easy to use interface at your fingertips.
